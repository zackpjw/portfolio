Bookmark

Built for digital natives, Bookmark provides a clean & easy interface for organizing your favourite websites!

Quick, fast access to your sites

Having to close & reopen your websites is a headache. We understand bad habits are hard to break. That is why Bookmark is here to make it great.

All of your favourite sites, store in one click

Bookmark is a simple manager built to help you manage your favourite websites all at the click of a button.

It remembers the amount of time you had spent on your sites and even suggests you to bookmark them.

Compatible on all web platforms

I had the pleasure of working with the team at Bookmark. After a period of analyzing and strategizing, we got to work creating a solid, minimalistic website with unique shape elements that would speak to the target audience.
