Insured

Insured needed a high-quality, innovative website to complement their personal & business insurance solutions.

Comprehensive coverage up to 95%!

Insured wanted to emphasize that buying insurance is good. With life, health, and general insurance, their clients are fully covered.

Online Insurer with fast processing claims!

In order to acquire clients for their services, the Insured team wanted them to have a great user experience.

We went through a few design concepts. Purple represents compassion. The curved, contour lines suggest comfort and ease. By integrating the two elements together, we are able to come up with a unique design which provides a pleasant and friendly user experience.
