Website design

October 2022

Yea Won

A beautiful website for the cosy restaurant from a small, northern town located down in Asia.

Satisfied bellies, happy smiles

From the screen of your device to right in front of you.

Yea Won wanted a website that displays their scrumptious, authentic, korean food that keeps guests drooling (and eating) more.

Authenticity that hits with nostalgia

An early version of their menu was up and running, but it desperately needed a makeover. Yea Won's request: Increase usability and user engagement.

The swan is a white, graceful bird that lives on the peaceful rivers of Korea. By integrating these majestic birds with Korean architecture, this presented a more pleasing and consistent visual design.

The process

Other design considerations: clean line items, geometric shapes, and soothing colors merged professional and approachable in the look and feel.
